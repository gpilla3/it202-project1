Game Genres

These are the game genres we are skilled with.

Action-Adventure

We can create Action-Adventure Games that includes video games that combine core elements from the action and adventure genres

Multiplayer

We can create Multiplayer Games that allows more than one person to play the same game environment at the same time, either locally or over the internet

Shooter

We can create Shooter Games that can test the player's speed and reaction time, while also providing enjoyment

Racing

We can create Racing Games where the player partakes in a racing competition with any type of land, water, air or space vehicles. They can be based on anything from real-world racing leagues to entirely fantastical settings

Fighting

We can create Fighting Games that are based around interpersonal combat between a limited amount of characters in which they fight until they defeat their opponents or the timer expires

RPG

We can create Role Playing Games in which players assume the roles of characters in a fictional setting and they take responsibility for acting out these roles within a narrative, either through literal acting or through a process of structured decision-making of character development

About

Here is our story!

  • 1982-1986

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We were 3 different people that met while studying game design at MIT, while getting our bachelors, the 3 of us started to do projects together out of our own self interest. We all enjoyed working together and we had the same persistence in completing projects. We fit together like a puzzle!

  • 1987 - 1994

    An Agency is Born

    After graduation from MIT, we started the company working on 16-bit games, we created many of the games you many know today, such as Megaman, Mortal Kombat as well as Super Mario!

  • December 2000

    Transition to Full Service

    As time went on we decided to take contracts from outside sources to create games for them, which ended up being an AMAZING idea as it got us to where we are today!

  • July 2005

    Phase Two Expansion

    As we created more games and started to get noticed by other companies and received more games to create we started to expand and bring in more developers to focus on more projects!

  • Now

    A family

    Now we are known for the amazing games we have made and the perfection we add to every project that comes out way. Our persistance and hardwork has been multiplied since we started and every here at Project RED work together as a family!
