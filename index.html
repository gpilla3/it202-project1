About

Here is our story!

  • 1982-1986

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We were 3 different people that met while studying game design at MIT, while getting our bachelors, the 3 of us started to do projects together out of our own self interest. We all enjoyed working together and we had the same persistence in completing projects!

  • 1987 - 1994

    An Agency is Born

    After graduation from MIT, we started the company working on 16-bit games, we created man of the game you many know today, such as Megaman, Mortal Kombat as well as Super Mario!

  • December 2012

    Transition to Full Service

    As time went on we decided to take contracts from outside sources to create games for them, which ended up being an AMAZING idea as it got us to where we are today!
